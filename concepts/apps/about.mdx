Vincent Apps enable trusted automation on behalf of users without compromising custody or control. Apps leverage Vincent's delegation framework to execute on-chain and off-chain actions within user-defined boundaries.

## Key Features

- **Policy-governed execution:** Every app must respect user-defined policies
- **Non-custodial design:** Users retain full control of assets
- **Cross-chain automation:** Works seamlessly across Solana, EVM chains, and Bitcoin
- **Auditable actions:** All operations are transparent and verifiable on-chain
- **Flexible integration:** Build autonomous agents or enhance existing web2/web3 apps
- **No lock-in:** Open-source framework with portable implementations
