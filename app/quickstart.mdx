## Creating a New Vincent App

This section of the Quick Start will cover the steps to create a new Vincent App and the minimum possible steps to execute an ability on behalf of a user.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into the Vincent Developer Dashboard">
    Go to the [Vincent Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.heyvincent.ai) and log in with your preferred authentication method. [TODO: ADD IMAGE]
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Vincent App">
    After logging in, click the **Create an app** button to access the [Create App Page](https://dashboard.heyvincent.ai/developer/create-app) [TODO: ADD IMAGE]
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the App Details">
    You can use test values for:
    - App Name
    - Contact Email
    - Description
    - App User URL
    - Redirect URIs

    The following field requires specific values:
    - **Delegatee Addresses**: This can be any EOA you have access to the private key of.

    Once you've filled in the required fields, click the **Create App** button to create your new Vincent App.

    <Info>
    If you misinput any of these, don't worry. These are editable after app creation as well.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Vincent Abilities">
    After creating your Vincent App, you'll be directed to the Add Abilities page. Click the **Add Abilities to Version** button, and select:
    - **ERC20 Approval**
    - **Uniswap Swap**

    to add them to your Vincent App. [TODO: ADD IMAGE]
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Version 1 as Active">
    Set version 1 of your application as the active version. This is not set as a default to prevent users from seeing your app before you're ready to release it. [TODO: ADD IMAGE]
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish the App Version">
    On the left sidebar, click **Apps**, then **App Versions**, and **Version 1** to navigate to the App Version management page. Click the **Publish App Version** button to register the App Version as published in the Vincent Registry smart contract. [TODO: ADD IMAGE]
  </Step>

  <Step title="Permit your App">
    On your App page, click the share button to view the Connect Page URL. Navigate to your App's connect page, and grant permissions to your application. [TODO: ADD IMAGE]
  </Step>
