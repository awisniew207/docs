title: Updating Abilities
---

Updated Vincent Abilities need to be added to our database before they can be accessible to application developers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into the Vincent Developer Dashboard">
    Go to the [Vincent Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.heyvincent.ai) and log in with your preferred authentication method. [TODO: ADD IMAGE]
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Your Vincent Ability">
    After logging in, navigate to your ability through the sidebar menu. [TODO: ADD IMAGE]
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Version">
    <ParamField path="Version" type="string" required>
      The new Ability version you'd like to add to the Vincent database.
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="Changes" type="string" required>
      Changes made to the ability since the previous version.
    </ParamField>

    <Note>This version must be published to `npm`. The `changes` field is editable.</Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
