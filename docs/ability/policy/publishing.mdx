---
title: Publishing Policies
---

Vincent Policies need to be published to our database before they can be accessible to application developers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into the Vincent Developer Dashboard">
    Go to the [Vincent Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.heyvincent.ai) and log in with your preferred authentication method.

    <img
      src="/images/policy/publishing/image-1.png"
      alt="Vincent Developer Dashboard login"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Vincent Policy">
    After logging in, click the **Create a policy** button to access the [Create Policy Page](https://dashboard.heyvincent.ai/developer/create-policy).

    <img
      src="/images/policy/publishing/image-2.png"
      alt="Create policy button"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the Policy Details">
    Complete the form with your policy information:

    <img
      src="/images/policy/publishing/image-3.png"
      alt="Policy details form"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />

    <ParamField path="Package Name" type="string" required>
      The published `npm` package name
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="Description" type="string" required>
      The policy description
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="Title" type="string" required>
      The policy title
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="Logo" type="image" required>
      Image of your policy shown to users during delegation
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="Active Version" type="string" required>
      The policy version which will be used by app creators
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="Deployment Status" type="string">
      The current status of the policy
    </ParamField>

    <Note>This version must be published to `npm`. Aside from the package name, all other metadata is editable.</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Policy Successfully Created">
    Your policy has been successfully published to the Vincent database and is now available for app developers to use.

    <img
      src="/images/policy/publishing/image-4.png"
      alt="Policy creation success confirmation"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Updating Your Policy

When you want to release a new version of your policy, you'll need to update our database with the new version information. We'll cover the version update process in the next section.
