---
title: Updating Abilities
---

Updated Vincent Abilities need to be added to our database before they can be accessible to application developers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into the Vincent Developer Dashboard">
    Go to the [Vincent Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.heyvincent.ai) and log in with your preferred authentication method.

    <img
      src="/images/ability/updating/image-1.png"
      alt="Vincent Developer Dashboard login"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Your Vincent Ability">
    After logging in, navigate to your ability through the sidebar menu.

    <img
      src="/images/ability/updating/image-2.png"
      alt="Navigate to ability in sidebar"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Ability Version">
    Click the **Create Ability Version** button to create a new version of your existing ability.

    <img
      src="/images/ability/updating/image-3.png"
      alt="Create Ability Version button"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in Version Details">
    Complete the form with your new version information:

    <img
      src="/images/ability/updating/image-4.png"
      alt="Version details form"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />

    <ParamField path="Version" type="string" required>
      The new Ability version you'd like to add to the Vincent database.
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="Changes" type="string" required>
      Changes made to the ability since the previous version.
    </ParamField>

    <Note>This version must be published to `npm`. The `changes` field is editable.</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Version Successfully Created">
    Your new ability version has been successfully added to the Vincent database and is now available for app developers to use.

    <img
      src="/images/ability/updating/image-5.png"
      alt="Version creation success confirmation"
      className="rounded-lg"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>
